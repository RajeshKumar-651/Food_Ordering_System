Our Famous Dishes

Kerala Special
Kerala Fried Prawns

This dish unearths the richness of the 'land down under', its marvelous seafood and its beautiful spices. Juicy prawns fried crisp in coconut oil, spluttering mustard seeds, crackling curry leaves, turmeric, fiery chillies and a drop of lemon.

Virudhunagar special
Virudhunagar Oil Parotta

Virudhunagar is famous for it’s oil partotta, where you get the flaky textured oil parottas that are shallow fried. As you crush it, it breaks down to beautiful crumbles.
